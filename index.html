Welcome to the MIND Lab

Research Focus: Our team is dedicated to applying machine learning and deep learning technologies to the intelligent analysis of multimodal medical images and physiological signals, integrating both software and hardware research to develop objective, reproducible, and interpretable clinical assistance systems.

Key Research Areas:  We have developed multiple advanced artificial intelligence models that accurately segment and grade lesions for survival prediction, quantitatively analyze traditional Chinese medicine tongue images, and synchronize pathological and physiological data via end-to-end bowel-sound acquisition and temporal modeling. By fusing temporal dynamics with graph-topology features in a multi-scale spatiotemporal network, we extract functional near-infrared spectroscopy patterns to distinguish psychiatric disorders, while deep-learning and multimodal fusion on CT/MRI enable tumor-growth forecasting and precise prognostication.
