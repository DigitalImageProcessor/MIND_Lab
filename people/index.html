Lab Leadership
Jing Zhang , Chief Scientist, Professor

Professor Jing Zhang has been engaged in various research, teaching, design, development and management work related to artificial intelligence, medical signal and image processing. As a key participant or technical leader, he has undertaken multiple research and technology breakthrough projects in Singapore on several occasions. He has published over a hundred papers in international journals and conferences, with several dozen being indexed by SCI and EI. He has also obtained two international patents and over ten Chinese patents. He has been awarded the Sichuan Provincial Talent Program Innovation and Entrepreneurship Team and the Chengdu Municipal Talent Program Innovation and Entrepreneurship Team, among others. In China, he has led one National Natural Science Foundation of China general project, one sub-project of the 863 Program, one project for returned overseas students from the Ministry of Education, two projects from the Sichuan Provincial Department of Science and Technology, and many other vertical and horizontal projects.

Xiao Han , Chief Scientist，Professor

Professor Xiao Han has long been devoted to advancements in machine learning technology within the medical field, including neuroscience, radiation therapy and computational pathology. Prior to joining Sichuan University, he served as a principal scientist at Tencent AI Lab and deputy general manager of Tencent AI Medical Center, where he led a team of over 30 researchers in medical AI research and product development. Among his key achievements, his team developed the Tencent AIMIS Digital Pathology Platform, which obtained EU CE certification in 2022 and won the Advanced Technology Achievement Award at the 2022 China International Big Data Industry Expo. In collaboration with Mindray Medical, his team developed China’s first AI-assisted peripheral blood cell analysis product – the first AI product in the in vitro diagnostic industry that was granted the “Green Pathway” for innovative medical devices by China NMPA. Professor Han has published over 100 papers in top international journals and leading conferences, with a total of over 12,000 citations (per Google Scholar). For the past five years, he has been consistently included in Stanford University’s list of the world’s top 2% scientists in both the "Career Scientific Impact" and "Annual Scientific Impact" categories. Professor Han holds over 40 granted patents in China and over 30 in the USA. He was awarded the China National Excellence Patent Award in 2022, and two Gold Prizes at Tencent’s Annual Patent Awards in 2022 and 2023 respectively.

Lab Leads
Ling He , Associate professor

Associate Professor Ling He has long been engaged in the fundamental and applied research of medical signal and image processing. He has led or participated in over ten projects including those funded by the National Natural Science Foundation of China, the Ministry of Education, the Science and Technology Department of Sichuan Province, and industry-related topics. He has published over 30 papers as the first or corresponding author, with more than 20 of them indexed by SCI/EI journals. He holds several Chinese patents. He has been awarded as an outstanding advisor in the China "Internet Plus" Innovation Competition and the National Biomedical Engineering Design Competition. He has consecutively received the Teaching Excellence Award, the Outstanding Mentor for Extracurricular Scientific Practice, and the Outstanding Award for Student Innovation Education from Sichuan University.

Zhonglan Tang , Associate professor

Associate Professor Zhonglan Tang has long been engaged in research and teaching in the fields of biomedical engineering and regulatory science for medical devices. She has undertaken and participated in numerous national and provincial-level scientific research projects, and has been involved in the first, second, and third batches of projects under the "China Regulatory Science Action Plan" of the National Medical Products Administration. She is a member of the Clinical Trials Research Professional Committee of the Chinese Society for Biomaterials, a member of the Regulatory Science Research Professional Committee of the China Association for Medical Device Regulation, an external expert of the Medical Device Evaluation Center of the National Medical Products Administration, a medical device clinical trial inspector of the Sichuan Provincial Medical Products Administration, and an external expert of the Chongqing Medical Device Technical Evaluation Center.

Gang Yang , Associate professor

Associate Professor Gang Yang has long been engaged in research on the bioelectromagnetic effects of stem cells and their induced differentiation, as well as the regeneration and repair of myocardial tissue with biomaterials. He has led or participated in numerous key projects of the National Natural Science Foundation of China, projects of the provincial science and technology department, and horizontal projects commissioned by enterprises. He has published over ten SCI papers and obtained multiple invention and utility model patents. He has also served as the chief editor of nine ordinary higher education textbooks, two of which are national-level planning textbooks for higher education institutions during the "11th Five-Year Plan" period.
