Postdoctoral Position Available

UTC+8

Our research group is committed to innovation in artificial intelligence-driven precision medical technology, focusing on four major areas: computational pathology, multimodal medical imaging analysis, intelligent medical signal interpretation, and intelligent medical device development. We aim to establish a comprehensive “algorithm-hardware-clinical” research framework. By developing interpretable AI models, adaptive analysis frameworks, and low-cost monitoring devices, we address clinical challenges in cancer diagnosis, disease prediction, and intraoperative monitoring. We sincerely invite postdoctoral researchers to join our interdisciplinary medical-engineering innovation team to collectively achieve the mission of “reducing medical costs and enhancing diagnostic/therapeutic accessibility,” while benefiting from academic development pathways and industry transformation resources.

Disciplinary Background Requirements:

Biomedical Engineering, Computer Science, Electronic Information, or related disciplines.

Position Requirements:

1、Meet Sichuan University’s postdoctoral recruitment requirements; must conduct postdoctoral research full-time, on-site.

2、Under 35 years of age.

3、Hold a doctoral degree obtained within the past three years.

4、Possess a solid research foundation and strong Chinese/English writing skills; have published at least two high-impact papers in medical imaging or signal processing.

5、Demonstrate independent research capabilities, strong self-motivation, responsibility, proactive work attitude, and excellent teamwork spirit.

Application Materials:

1、Personal CV (File name: Name-CV-Intended Supervisor)

2、Recommendation letter from doctoral advisor

3、Representative publications, monographs, patents, or awards reflecting the applicant’s highest academic achievements

Compensation: To be discussed in person.

Application: Interested applicants should submit their CV to: Professor Han Xiao， Email: xiao_han@scu.edu.cn
