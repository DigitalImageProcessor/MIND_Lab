Research Assistant Position Available

UTC+8

Joint Research Group of Professor Zhang Jing and Professor Han Xiao at Sichuan University School of Biomedical Engineering, Now Recruiting Research Assistants. We invite applications from candidates with backgrounds in Biomedical Engineering or related fields, particularly those proficient in medical image processing and medical signal processing technologies.

Disciplinary Background Requirements: Biomedical Engineering, Computer Science, Electronic Information, or related disciplines.

Position Requirements:

1、Advanced English Literature Proficiency: Demonstrated ability to efficiently and accurately comprehend cutting-edge international research literature.

2、Strong Programming Skills: Proficiency in at least one mainstream programming language (e.g., Python, C++), with solid capabilities in code implementation, debugging, and optimization.

3、Foundational Algorithm Expertise: Solid theoretical grounding and practical experience in core algorithms for medical signal processing and/or medical image processing, with proven ability to solve real-world problems using these techniques.

4、Excellent Interpersonal and Team Skills: Strong communication abilities and a collaborative team spirit.

5、Accountability and Collaboration: Commitment to completing assigned tasks on schedule and working proactively with colleagues to achieve team objectives.

Education Requirement: Master’s degree or higher.

Compensation and Benefits:

1、Monthly salary: ¥5,000 – ¥10,000 (gross, before tax).

2、Qualified candidates will receive priority consideration for PhD admission within our research group, subject to meeting the university’s basic doctoral program requirements.

Application: Interested applicants should submit their CV to: Professor Han Xiao， Email: xiao_han@scu.edu.cn
